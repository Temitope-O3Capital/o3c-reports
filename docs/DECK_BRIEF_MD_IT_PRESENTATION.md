Your job is to impress them, walk them through every major feature in plain English, and secure their approval to complete the remaining development.

The tone is: **executive-polished + modern + grounded in Nigerian fintech context.** Confident, not boastful. No technical jargon whatsoever. Numbers and outcomes first. Think McKinsey meets a modern African fintech brand deck.

## DESIGN INSTRUCTIONS FOR CLAUDE

When building the PowerPoint with python-pptx, follow these rules:

### Slide master / defaults
- Slide background: white (`#FFFFFF`) unless specified as Navy or other
- All body text: 13–14pt, `#0F172A` on light slides, `#FFFFFF` on navy slides
- All headings: Sora Bold or Calibri Bold, navy (`#0E2841`) on light slides, white on dark slides
- Section labels: 9pt, ALL CAPS, letter-spacing wide, colour `#C00000`
- Slide number: bottom right, small, `#94A3B8`

### Accent elements
- Use thin left-border boxes (`4px solid #C00000`) for callout quotes
- Use sky blue (`#0EA5E9`) for metric/data callout boxes
- Use green (`#059669`) for "done" / positive callouts
- Use light grey (`#F4F6F8`) for card tile backgrounds on white slides

### Typography hierarchy
| Level | Size | Weight | Colour |
|-------|------|--------|--------|
| Cover title | 52pt | Bold | White |
| Slide headline | 32–40pt | Bold | Navy |
| Sub-headline | 22–28pt | SemiBold | Navy |
| Body | 13–14pt | Regular | `#0F172A` |
| Bullets | 13pt | Regular | `#0F172A` |
| Small / label | 9–10pt | SemiBold | `#64748B` or `#C00000` |

### Bullet style
- Use em-dash (—) or filled circle bullet, navy colour
- Max 4 bullets per column
- Line spacing: 1.4× within bullets, 0.5× space between bullets

### Cards / boxes
- Border radius: 12pt (use RoundedRectangle autoshape in pptx)
- Shadow: soft outer shadow, offset 2pt, blur 8pt, opacity 12%
- Padding: ~12–14pt inside

### Icons
- If using Material Symbols or emoji icons, keep them consistent across slides (same style)
- Icon colour: `#C00000` (red) for action icons, `#0EA5E9` (blue) for data icons, `#059669` (green) for success icons

### Slide transitions
- Use Fade (0.3s) between slides
- No animations on individual elements — keep it clean and fast

---

## WHAT NOT TO DO

- Do NOT use technical terms: no "API", "endpoint", "migration", "TypeScript", "React", "backend"
